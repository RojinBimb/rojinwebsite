How technology has affected us

We rely on technology far too much. There is no denying it. Many businesses are unable to function if their internet connection fails. Many businesses and institutions are left high and dry if the internet or computer crashes.Every piece of company information is input into the computer. Without the ability to send and receive emails, how will we be able to conduct business? Without the internet, how would anyone be able to learn about a subject? This is how awful it has gotten.This demonstrates how reliant we are on technology. We have forgotten how humans obtained knowledge prior to the invention of computers. We've lost sight of how business used to be done before conference calls and emails. Now that technology has taken over our life, it has an obvious impact on our creativity and communication. First and foremost, creativity is critical to the advancement of our civilization and our way of living. New breakthroughs make our lives easier, allowing us to do things we couldn't previously, as well as creating jobs and causing the economy to boom. From an art gallery to our mobile phones, which give us with knowledge and capability that previous generations could only dream of, to the way we purchase on the internet and the new digital economy we live in, creativity and innovation can be found everywhere.
